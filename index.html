My love of Golf through the years

Introduction to the game

I do not remember exactly how old I was when I first began playing golf, but it was somewhere between 8 and 10 years old. My brother and I would get the chance to play with my dad. My dad had just begun playing so we all sort of started learning together. My brother and I were terrible and my dad was slightly less terrible. We played at some courses in St. Peters and some at the Lake of the Ozarks. The course I remember at the lake was Bay View Golf Course.

The High School Years

When I got old enough to work, I began working at a local golf course. I was able to play golf for free so I was able to increase my skills. I played as often as possible, especially during the summers. The golf course I worked at was called "Emerald Greens Golf Course" and recently was closed because the property was bought by the St. Louis Zoo.

The College Years

During college I continued to work at Emerald Greens while I was off on summer breaks. I would often work a morning and then play golf in the afternoon. Some friends of mine who also worked there would also play with me and we would often play 2 vs 2. I got some new clubs in the summer of 2009 and I still have them.

The Recent Years

I have been able to play much less golf in the last few years but still get the chance to in the summer. I got a chance to play at a course in Pebble Beach, California in June of 2018. Pebble Beach is a famous golf community with extremely expensive courses but I was able to play for free because a friend was working at the course at the time. The course is called Poppy Hills. Interesting Facts

I worked at Bellerive Country Club in the summer of 2018 and the PGA Championship was played there. It was a really neat experience and I got to be up close to a lot of the pros.
